Genie One will soon be available to account-only users
> [翻訳エラー: Error code: 400 - {'type': 'error', 'error': {'type': 'invalid_request_error', 'message': 'Your credit balance is too low to access the Anthropic API. Please go to Plans & Billing to upgrade or purchase credits.'}, 'request_id': 'req_011CdGzE25yXzMjWmVD4NiRS'}]
July 21, 2026

Genie One will soon support account-only users, allowing any user in your identity provider to access Genie One without being assigned to a workspace or granted the consumer entitlement. When enabled, all users in your identity provider are enrolled automatically. Unity Catalog will continue to govern what each user can see.

This feature will be in Public Preview and enabled by default. To be eligible, accounts must enable Automatic Identity Management (or account-level SCIM) and a custom URL.

Account admins can restrict access with AIM access boundaries or disable account-only access in the account console.
